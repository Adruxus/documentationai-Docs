## Overview

Keep your documentation tidy and accessible by organizing projects.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Click "New Project" on your dashboard and enter a name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documentation pages">
    Use the editor to add or import pages with Markdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Group by sections">
    Create sections (e.g. Setup, API) for easier navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    Use the "Share" option to add team members to your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
