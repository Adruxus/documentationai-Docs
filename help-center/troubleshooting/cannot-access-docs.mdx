---
title: Cannot Access Documentation
description: Fix issues if you can't view your documentation.
---

## Symptoms

- "Page Not Found" errors
- Stuck on loading screen

## Causes

- Not logged in
- Incorrect URL
- Permissions issue

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login">
    Try logging out and logging back in at `https://chadhansen.com/login`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify URL">
    Make sure the page address is correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask your admin">
    If issues persist, check that you have permission to view the docs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
