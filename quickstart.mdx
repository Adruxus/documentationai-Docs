## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up and access VFX Studios.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Navigate to the [VFX Studios Dashboard](https://setlist-storyboard-creator.base44.app/Dashboard).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click the `Sign Up` button and enter your email address and password.
    
    Alternatively, use Google or Apple sign-in for faster access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your credentials, and log in to access your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generate Your First AI Visual

Creating your first asset is simple and takes under 2 minutes.

<Tabs default-tab="2">
  <Tab title="AI Prompt" icon="sparkles">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter Prompt" icon="edit-3">
        In the Asset Creator, type a description like "neon waves reacting to upbeat electronic music".
      </Step>
      <Step title="Customize" icon="sliders">
        Adjust mood, colors (`#22C55E` for brand green), and duration.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate" icon="zap">
        Click `Generate` and watch AI create your visual in seconds.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Audio Reactive" icon="music">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Upload Audio" icon="upload">
        Drag your music track into the audio-reactive zone.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Style" icon="palette">
        Choose from presets like "Pulsing Bass" or "Melodic Flow".
      </Step>
      <Step title="Preview & Export" icon="play">
        Preview live and export as MP4 or GIF.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
Save your asset to the library for easy access in setlists or storyboards. Use the share button to get an embed code for your site.
</Callout>
